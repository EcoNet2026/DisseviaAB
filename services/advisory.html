Project Communication and Strategic Support

EcoNet AB assists projects and organisations with professional project communication, including:

  • Target group analysis
  • Stakeholder mapping
  • Business intelligence and contextual analysis

These services aim to:

  • Increase visibility
  • Strengthen project impact
  • Maximise societal benefit
  • Support effective communication towards funders, partners, and the public
