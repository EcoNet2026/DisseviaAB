Events, Conferences, and Meeting Places

EcoNet AB offers services for planning, arranging, and conducting events in Sweden, including:

  • Conferences
  • Workshops
  • Trainings
  • Seminars
  • networking Events

The purpose of these activities is to create meeting places that promote collaboration between academia, business, and the public sector, with a particular focus on information and IT-related fields.

Event-related services include:

  • Event and conference planning
  • Programme design
  • Moderator and facilitation support
  • Logistics and coordination
  • Communication before, during, and after events
  • Administration of participants, speakers, and partners
