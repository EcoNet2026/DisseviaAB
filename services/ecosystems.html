National Ecosystem and Network Development

The company works with support for national ecosystem development within Swedish research, innovation, and digitalization.

Activities include:

  • Mapping relevant actors, organizations, and environments in Sweden
  • Identifying collaboration needs and gaps
  • Establishing and supporting networks
  • Advising on strategic collaborations
  • Facilitating contacts between:
    • Research organisations
    • Industrial partners
    • Public sector organisations

The objective is to:

  • Strengthen knowledge transfer
  • Increase innovation capacity
  • Improve national coordination within relevant sectors
