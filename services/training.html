Training, Skills Development, and Advisory Activities

The company also conducts training and skills development activities, including:

  • Workshops
  • Lectures
  • Courses
  • Training Programmes
  • Production of educational and support materials

These activities aim to strengthen the ability of Swedish organisations to:

  • Communicate effectively
  • Build and manage collaborations
  • Implement strategies and digital tools
  • Work efficiently in projects and innovation environments
