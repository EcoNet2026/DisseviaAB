Communication & Dissemination Services

1. Communication and Dissemination Services

EcoNet AB provides professional communication and dissemination services for externally financed projects in Sweden.

  • Development of communication and dissemination strategies
  • Planning and execution of dissemination activities tailored to relevant target groups
  • Production of content, including:
    • Texts and articles
    • Reports and analyses
    • Visual and graphic material
    • Presentations and communication materials
    • Digital content, websites, and social media content

The dissemination activities are aimed at relevant stakeholders and target groups within Swedish research, innovation, and technology environments.
